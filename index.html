MealMate

MealMate is an Android app designed to help users organize and plan their meals efficiently. The app offers a wide variety of recipes that users can explore based on their dietary preferences and restrictions. Whether you're looking for gluten-free, vegetarian, or other types of meals, MealMate ensures you can find the perfect recipe for your needs. Some of the key features of MealMate include:

  • Recipe Browsing: Users can browse through a variety of recipes and filter them based on specific dietary restrictions such as vegetarian, gluten-free, etc.
  • Meal Planner: The app allows users to plan their meals for the week ahead, making it easier to stick to a healthy eating schedule.
  • Grocery List: MealMate automatically generates a grocery list based on the meals users select, saving time and ensuring they have all the necessary ingredients.
  • Favorite Recipes: Users can save their favorite recipes for quick access, making meal planning more convenient.
  • Ingredient Management: From any recipe, users can add ingredients directly to their grocery list, simplifying the shopping process.
  • Personalized Meal Tracker: The app helps users track their calorie intake and monitor their meals, supporting healthier eating habits.
With MealMate, you can streamline your meal planning, ensure you stay on top of your nutrition, and make grocery shopping much more efficient.

SuitYourself

SuitYourself was developed as part of an all-female hackathon with the theme of benefiting women. The app uses cutting-edge AI to help women build and manage a wardrobe that adapts to their unique needs and preferences. Leveraging a combination of powerful APIs, including Google Calendar, Google Cloud Vision, a Menstrual Cycle API, and a Weather API, SuitYourself intelligently suggests outfits based on the userâ€™s calendar events, the weather forecast, and even their menstrual cycle, ensuring that the user is always dressed appropriately and comfortably. Key features include:

  • AI-Powered Wardrobe Creation: Using Google Cloud Vision, the app scans clothing items and categorizes them, helping users build a digital wardrobe they can access anytime.
  • Weather-Driven Outfit Suggestions: The app pulls real-time weather data through a Weather API, ensuring that users are always dressed appropriately for the day.
  • Menstrual Cycle Integration: By integrating a Menstrual Cycle API, the app provides tailored outfit suggestions based on the user's menstrual cycle, promoting comfort during different phases.
  • Google Calendar Sync: SuitYourself syncs with your Google Calendar to suggest outfits based on upcoming events, meetings, or activities.
  • Interactive Chatbot: The app includes an OpenAI-powered chatbot that answers user queries about their wardrobe and offers style advice.
This app aims to help women optimize their wardrobes, save time, and feel confident in their outfit choices, no matter the occasion..
